Credit Risk Assessment

Predict default risk in seconds. Smarter decisions for lenders and analysts.

What it does

This tool helps lenders and credit analysts quickly assess whether an applicant is likely to default. Enter applicant and loan details once, and get a clear Default or Non-Default prediction powered by a trained CatBoost model.

Features

Fast prediction

Get a result as soon as you submit the form—no waiting.

Multiple factors

Age, income, loan details, and credit history in one flow.

Clear outcome

Binary Default / Non-Default so you can act with confidence.

Responsive

Use it on desktop, tablet, or phone—layout adapts to you.
